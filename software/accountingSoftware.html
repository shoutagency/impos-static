Find out more about ImPOS
and OneSaas

Via OneSaas ImPOS integrates with more accounting programs than any other Australian POS vendor. At the click of your mouse you can pull sales data straight out of ImPOS and straight into your accounting program. Smooth as butter. 

Major accounting platforms covered include Quickbooks, Sage One, Saasu, KashFlow, Xero plus MYOB. 
 
It is all so simple. Synchronise your sales data to your accounting software without the headache of exporting and importing CSV files.  OneSaas is so simple, it syncs all your data across every evening to your accounting file without you needing to be involved. 
Like an accountants assistant, this tool will save hours of your days entering information from one system to another, and on top of that, accuracy from the systems is assured.  No more manually entering the days takings. 
 
Save hours per week. Save money on bookkeepers, save your sanity! 
About OneSaas
OneSaas - Cloud Integrations Made Easy allows you to Seamlessly integrate and synchronize your data across a large number of cloud and on-premise platforms. OneSaas can integrate multiple services centralizing all your contacts, invoices, orders, products and other business data.

ImPOS only partners with industry leading applications and OneSaas is another example of us offering you access to cutting edge technology.
