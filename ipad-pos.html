ImPAD & Tablet Ordering – iPad Ordering

ImPOS offers the option of using popular consumer grade tablets such as the iPad and Mini iPad. You can use your existing iPad. All ImPOS needs to do is stage the iPad and you'll be up and running in no time. Using an iPad or Windows based tablet in enables you to take an order directly at the table, cash off a table or start a new bar tab wirelessly. You can increase interaction with your customers with options to show pictures of food or beverage including any tasting notes from the chef or sommelier.

iPad's are becoming a popular option for venues that want to add that additional waiter station to the floor. ImPOS can easily run an iPad mounted to a wall bracket or bench or even to process orders directly at the table. As far as the user interface is concerned, it is business as usual, meaning no additional training is required.

By removing the ‘dead time’ typically involved in writing down an order and entering it at the nearest point of sale terminal, you increase efficiency and service to your customers. Venues are commenting on how refreshing it is to spend more time with the customers and less time running around the venue.
