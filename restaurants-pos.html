Restaurant Point of Sale

ImPOS is a hospitality specific Point of Sale system offering the most superior in restaurant POS solutions – not a retail based system that has been altered to work in the hospitality space. This is critical to the fundamentals behind ImPOS. Installing ImPOS ensures that you can manage your restaurant efficiently and effectively.

Plus many, many more...

Detailed live web-based reporting is such a strong restaurant POS feature working from PCs, iPhones and iPads allows you to review all aspects of the business with ease. Imagine being able to sit at home or even on holiday and view what is selling and what is not selling! Critical reporting includes sales margin analysis, staff sales, time attendance with biometric facilities and many more. Our popular Weekly Revenue report gives management the tools to evaluate the business operations effectively in a two page report.

A detailed Stock management module allows you to track all purchases and sales information. ImSTOCK is simple to use and an essential partner for any restaurant serious about managing their business.

While we provide all the possible restaurant POS information you could ever want at your fingertips, IMPOS prides themselves on offering exemplary customer service around the clock and are committed to providing genuine care and support throughout our working relationship.
