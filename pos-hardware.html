POS Hardware

The highest quality hardware - Means more reliable systems

At ImPOS we offer a range of touch-screen hardware options to suit the hard-core demands of the hospitality environment. We don’t take short cuts or skimp on quality because if you experience hardware failure during service it costs you $$$$. Where other POS companies settle for second best or fail to offer a commercial grade product, we choose only the hardware that will keep you running – day and night, 365 days of the year.

The main touch screen products we implement are world leading commercial hardware specific products branded Advanpos and Puritron. In the tablet space, Motion are our preferred suppliers. All touch screen terminals supplied by Advanpos and Puritron utilise fanless technology and most have a minimum IP rating (waterproofness) of 54 – meaning they are dust and splash proof.



Z-LITE (Flat Screen POS)

Enter the ultimate terminal for bar/nightclub environment. This unit is similar in specifications to the wall mounted unit in terms of durability. Our clear winner for any bar or wet environment due to no air vents at all and the durable casing that makes up the unit. We now offer this as standard dual-core which ensures that speed is always optimized both now and in to the future.

W-POS

Constructed of hardened die-cast magnesium alloy these units are very durable and look great. Wall mount terminals can either be fixed to a wall directly, on a swivel bracket or even on an easy to remove bracket to suit an outside application where you need to remove the terminal to relocate inside at the end of trade. They can also be pole mounted and can provide a 360 rotation to be utilised from each side of the counter. A great idea for any venue that is tight for space is to pole mount the units, and mount the cash drawers under the counter/bar to free up space.

E-POS

Our entry level range but still boasting a 3 year manufacturer backed warranty. The EPOS is our most versatile unit, able to be wall-mounted, pole-mounted or counter placed.

Motion Tablet

A rugged, take anywhere, do anything option – the Motion is the ideal tablet for busy bars, wet areas, outdoor areas or anywhere that might be exposed to the hospitality elements. A premium option that gives venues maximum flexibility, allowing the motion to be easily moved from one area to another depending on time and type of service you are in.

iPad + Mini iPad

ImPOS offers the option of using popular consumer grade tablets such as the iPad and Mini iPad. You can use your existing iPad and create an iPad POS. All ImPOS needs to do is stage the iPad and you’ll be up and running in no time.

Peripherals

We stock a complete range of popular POS equipment like barcode scanners, receipt printers and cash drawers. We offer all the leading peripheral brands including Puritron, Epson, AdvanPOS, Posiflex, Fujitsu, Cisco and more.
